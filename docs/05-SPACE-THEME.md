# 05 - Space Theme & Level Destinations

## 1. Thematic Overview

The game maps mathematical progression to a cosmic journey. Each of the 4 operations represents a **Galaxy** (region of space), and each level within that galaxy corresponds to a **destination** — a planet, moon, star, or celestial object. The further the child progresses, the deeper into space they travel.

```
EARTH (Home) ──────────────────────────────────────────────►
  Galaxy 1: Solar System (Addition)
    Galaxy 2: Outer Reaches (Subtraction)
      Galaxy 3: Nearby Stars (Multiplication)
        Galaxy 4: Deep Space (Division)
```

## 6. Visual Progression

### Background Transitions

As the player progresses through galaxies, the background evolves:

```
Galaxy 1 (Addition):
  - Start: Earth view, blue atmosphere, familiar
  - Middle: Dark space, individual planets visible
  - End: Edge of solar system, vast emptiness

Galaxy 2 (Subtraction):
  - Start: Interstellar space, scattered stars
  - Middle: Colorful nebulae appear
  - End: Dense star fields, approaching star systems

Galaxy 3 (Multiplication):
  - Start: Inside brilliant nebulae
  - Middle: Star clusters, stellar nurseries
  - End: Approaching galactic center, intense brightness

Galaxy 4 (Division):
  - Start: Between galaxies, cosmic void
  - Middle: Galaxy spirals everywhere
  - End: Cosmic web visible, approaching the origin of everything
```

### Ship Journey Animation

During each level:
1. **Start:** Ship at left side of screen, destination planet/star at right
2. **Progress:** Ship moves left-to-right proportionally (5% per correct answer)
3. **Background:** Parallax scrolling stars (3 layers at different speeds)
4. **Approach:** As ship nears destination, it grows larger and more detailed
5. **Landing:** Celebration animation upon level completion

### Parallax Layers

```
Layer 0 (farthest): Tiny distant stars, slow scroll
Layer 1 (middle):   Medium stars and dust, medium scroll
Layer 2 (nearest):  Bright stars and particles, fast scroll
Layer 3 (effects):  Occasional asteroids, comets, shooting stars
```
